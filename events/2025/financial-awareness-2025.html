Financial Awareness & Consumer Training

19 April 2025 | Completed Event

Event Description

The Department of Computer Applications organized an NCFE-sponsored workshop on Financial Awareness and Consumer Training for II Year MCA students. The session focused on financial literacy, responsible consumer behavior, and personal finance management using real-life examples.

Objectives

  • To improve financial literacy among students
  • To promote responsible consumer practices
  • To enhance financial decision-making skills

Outcomes

  • Students gained practical knowledge of personal finance
  • Improved awareness of budgeting and savings
  • Active participation and positive feedback
