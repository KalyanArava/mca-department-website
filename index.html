About MCA Department

The Department of Computer Applications (MCA) at SVCE is committed to providing quality education and practical exposure in the field of computer science and applications. The department focuses on developing technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and professional ethics among students.

During the academic year 2025, the department successfully organized various academic, technical, cultural, and outreach programs to enhance student learning and holistic development.

Department Highlights – 2025

Major completed events and activities

Financial Awareness Programs

Technical & Academic Events

Events like Tech Spark 2025, technical quizzes, and industry interaction sessions enhanced technical and professional skills.

Outreach & Social Activities

Innovation outreach programs, NSS activities under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, and community engagement initiatives were successfully conducted.
